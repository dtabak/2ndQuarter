Key point #1¶

Heating Load and Cooling Load are strongly correlated as seen in correlation matrix. The correlation between these two variables equals to 0.98. It is the highest value after diagonal ones. Key Point #3¶

Overall Height falls into two levels. It can be either 3.5 or 7. Heating Load and Cooling Load are higher when it is 7. That means more Heating Load and Cooling Load are required for the higher level of Overall Height.

In [23]:

Summary¶

In a nutshell, the key findings are as below.

  • Heating Load and Cooling Load are strongly positive correlated with each other.
  • Relative Compactness and Surface Area are strongly negative correlated with each other.
  • More Heating Load and Cooling Load are required for the higher level of Overall Height.
